Working with Z Scores

Problems:

Let us suppose that the average American eats 2000 calories per day. (μ = 2000) Furthermore, suppose that the standard deviation is 400, and the population is normally distributed.

  1. What percentage of people eat fewer than 2000 calories per day?


  2. What percentage of people eat more than 2000 calories per day?


  3. What percentage of people eat between 1600 and 2000 calories per day?


  4. What percentage of people eat between 2000 and 2400 calories per day?


  5. What percentage of people eat fewer than 1600 calories per day?


  6. What percentage of people eat more than 2400 calories per day?


  7. What percentage of people eat between 1200 and 2800 calories per day?


  8. What percentage of people eat fewer than 1200 calories per day?


  9. What percentage of people eat more than 2800 calories per day?


  10. What percentage of people eat fewer than 800 calories per day?


  11. What percentage of people eat fewer than 1800 calories per day?
